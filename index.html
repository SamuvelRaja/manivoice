Who am I

I am manimaran a seasoned Tamil voice-over artist and event professional with a passion for storytelling. My expertise spans audiobook production, podcasting, dubbing, and voice training, all in Tamil. Beyond the studio, I also specialize in hosting and anchoring events, ensuring each one is memorable and engaging for Tamil-speaking audiences.

I founded GT MANIDHAM from the love for the craft of communication and entertainment that i inherently have. With over 7 years of experience in the industry, our journey began with a commitment to delivering high-quality voice and event solutions.
